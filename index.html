Hello, I'm Chandler Azling

I'm a UI designer, programmer, and artist.

Hey, I'm Chandler and I'm from Orange County, California. I have a bachelor's degree in Computer Science from UC Berkeley and my area of specialty is UI/UX design. I love hiking, watching animation, and exploring new places. (I've lived in Japan twice!) I also really love art and programming, and this has been a personal dilemma for some time. Which do I pursue?

In high school, my dream was to become a concept artist at an animation studio. However, I also really liked math and valued academics, so I decided to go to a university where I could pursue art as well as something technical. I chose UC Berkeley and fell in love with computer science my first semester. I was accepted into the Art Practice and Computer Science majors, but as I worked towards both I began to question my career path.

Although I really liked both computer science and art, I felt like I needed to focus on just one for my career. I also threw Japanese classes in the mix which just made things even more confusing. I chose to drop my art major so that I could study abroad in Japan and still graduate in 4 years, but art was always still on my mind. I tried computer graphics, but somehow the combination of art, coding, and linear algebra (my favorite math) turned out to be an absolute nightmare.

It wasn't until my very last semester of college that I discovered UI/UX design, and that's when everything clicked for me. I finally found something where I could combine my creative side and my technical side. I'm passionate about creating intuitive designs and thinking from the user's perspective.
